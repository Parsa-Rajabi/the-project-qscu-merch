Since 2011, the Quantitative Science Course Union has been representing UBC Okanagan students in Mathematics, Computer Science, Statistics, and Physics and Data Science. The QSCU organizes both fun and educational events while also assisting first-year students in their courses. These events are usually held on campus. The course union strives to bring students and professors together in a casual atmosphere in hopes of creating social networking and research opportunities.
